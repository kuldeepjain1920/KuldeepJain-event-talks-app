# Future Improvements – Prioritized

1. **Persist Theme Preference** – Store light/dark mode in `localStorage` to retain across sessions.
2. **Toggle Visibility & Labelling** – Clear ARIA labels, dynamic text ("Switch to Dark Mode" / "Switch to Light Mode").
3. **Copy‑to‑Clipboard Feedback** – Toast/snackbar with auto‑dismiss, fallback messaging.
4. **Loading & Interaction States** – Spinner/placeholder for CSV export, disable button while processing.
5. **Error Handling & Messaging** – User‑friendly dialogs with retry options.
6. **Search Experience** – Clear button, highlight matches.
7. **Responsive Design Enhancements** – Stack header controls on small screens, larger tap targets.
8. **Accessibility (a11y)** – Proper roles/ARIA, focus outlines, logical tab order.
9. **Onboarding / Help** – Tooltip or modal guide on first visit.
10. **Performance Optimizations** – Lazy‑load images, cache releases in `sessionStorage`.
11. **Consistent Visual Language** – Unified button styles, no hard‑coded colors.
12. **Export CSV Enhancements** – Descriptive headers, optional JSON export.
13. **Tweet Composer UX** – Live character count color change, copy button, confirmation.
14. **Testing & Feedback Loop** – Small in‑app survey for real‑world data.
